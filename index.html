行為改變研究與理論

Behavior Change Research and Theory: Psychological and Technological Perspectives

本書由 Little、Sillence 與 Joinson 三位學者主編，匯集行為改變領域跨學科的前沿研究。 涵蓋心理學介入、行動健康、說服性科技、網路安全、數位追蹤等多元主題， 提供理論模型與實證案例的完整整合。

This edited volume brings together interdisciplinary perspectives on behavior change, spanning psychological theory, mobile health interventions, persuasive technology design, cybersecurity, and digital tracking. Each chapter offers a unique lens on how digital tools and behavioral science can be combined to promote positive change.

Elsevier Academic Press 2017 Little · Sillence · Joinson (Eds.) Psychology & HCI ISBN 978-0-12-802690-8
